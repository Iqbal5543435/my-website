About Us

Al Mansoor Agencies is a leading provider of mechanical and electrical services, machinery expertise, and pharmaceutical tools manufacturing. With over 30 years of industrial experience, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality maintenance, installation, and repair solutions for diverse industries, including pharmaceutical, food, textile, and commercial sectors. As a certified Government Contractor (KMC & ZMC), we specialize in HVAC systems, mechanical equipment, complete overhauling, preventive maintenance, and industrial troubleshooting. Our expert team of engineers and technicians operates both on-site and in our fully equipped workshop, ensuring the highest level of service and efficiency. We take pride in our long-standing relationships with clients, providing 24/7 support, cost-effective solutions, and innovative designs to meet evolving industry demands. At Al Mansoor Agencies, we are committed to excellence, reliability, and continuous development, making us the trusted choice for businesses seeking top-tier maintenance and engineering solutions.
